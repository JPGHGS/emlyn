It is a fantastic place to let of steam from a hard days work.

Rating: ****
The Hydro The Hydro is a sports facility with 3 swimiing pools, a multi-tier diving board up to 50 m. an olimpic size swimming pool. It has a gym and vast rooms for sport activitys. It also has a stadium style seeting on one side for compitisions and events

Opening Times: 7am-10pm/Mon-Fri

Rating: ****
Valley Gardens Valley gardens is a large park in the middle of Harrogate, it has many enterances. It contains a skate park for youths, and a crazy golf. There is also vast areas of grass where you can play sports.
